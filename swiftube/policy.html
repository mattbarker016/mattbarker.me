SwifTube Private Policy



SwifTube never stores any of your personal data or sensitive YouTube information. Ever. By default, SwifTube uses publically available data to load videos. If you choose to Sign In, SwifTube uses OAuth 2.0 to securely validate the request with Google. To access your subscription feed, SwifTube will View your YouTube account, which allows SwifTube to "View your videos, playlists, and YouTube activity". This is necessary to obtain the latest videos of the channels you are subscribed to. Upon signing out, SwifTube will reset and clear all tokens related to your account.
