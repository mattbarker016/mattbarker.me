SwifTube Private Policy





SwifTube never stores any of your personal data or sensitive YouTube information. Ever.

By default, SwifTube uses your phone's language and region preferences, as well as publicly accessible data, to load videos and search results. If you choose to Sign In, SwifTube uses OAuth 2.0 to securely validate the request with Google.

Only at this point will SwifTube view your YouTube account, which allows SwifTube to "view your videos, playlists, and YouTube activity" to generate your subscription feed. This is necessary to obtain the latest videos of the channels you are subscribed to. Upon signing out, SwifTube will reset and clear all tokens related to your account.
